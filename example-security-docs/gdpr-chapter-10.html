    GDPR Chapters with Sections & Articles

    GDPR is the acronym for the European Union General Data Protection Regulations .

    Chapter 10. Delegated Acts and Implementing Acts (Articles 92-93)

    Covers the delegation and implementing of acts.

    Article 92 – Exercise of the delegation
    The Commission has the power to adopt delegated acts. Delegation of power can be revoked at any time by the European Parliament or the Council.
    Article 93 – Committee procedure
    The Commission will be assisted by a committee.
